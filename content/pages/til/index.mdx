## Export GPG 
this is an example how to "backup" your "GPG/PGP":
- basic
```shell
❯ gpg  --armor --export <email/key-id> > my_secret.asc
```
- use `paperkey`
```shell
❯ gpg --export-secret-key <email/key-id> | paperkey --output my_secret.asc
```
